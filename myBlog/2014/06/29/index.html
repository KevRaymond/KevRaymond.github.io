This is Gonna Take Some Work

I am totally loving this outline but, I fishing in some new waters here. The fascination I have with technology and the internet is rivaled only by my love for business, sports and ladies. The order is ever changing.

Learning to use Fargo as a blogging platform is going to take some patience on my part. There will be some issues with the blog from time to time as I figure out the methods of writing some simple code, update the site, learn how to host it etc. etc. etc. However, I am up for the challenge.

Plus, I just watched an episode of Halt and Catch Fire, so I am completely ready to deal with any and all computing issues

Issue 1

  • Figuring out how to keep a new page from showing up in the blog stream. An example being the page called 'The Deets' at the bottom of this blog. Scroll to the bottom of the home page and you will see what I mean.

Issue 2

  • Getting a picture to show up from Google Drive.

Everything is going to be a work in progress and I will enjoy every minute. Granted, I have a business to run so it will be a slow go but, I want to finally have a blog I enjoy from all aspects. I have tried to blog many times, only to get bored and change everything around. Nothing ever seemed to work.

The goal is this. If I am going to succeed at having a blog people want to read I have to do it the right way, make the effort and learn along the way. This blog will be about my life, a chronicle of what I find interesting and journeys I will have. Creating the thing is obstacle 1 and will probably be a focus of the topics for some time.

I'm excited
