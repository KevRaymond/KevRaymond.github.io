The New Website is Up!

After a week of schizophrenic fits, I finally came to a conclusion regarding my personal website. All of this came about after discovering Fargo, my new favorite outliner/web publisher. Over the years my personal website/blogs have been disjointed and boring. Yes, this could happen again, but I hope not.

My new website is a link blog using Jeffrey Kisner's OPML to Wordpress plugin. The new domain is kevraymond.com and I decided to host the main site with Bluehost and use Wordpress, with which I have some basic skills. The blog is located at blog.kevraymond.com, where you are now and hosted via Github Pages so I can use Fargo.

I decided to go with kevraymond.com instead of my already owned kevinraymond.net because I hate .net's. And, whomever owns kevinraymond.com renews every year and doesn't even have a site on the domain. Enraging! The process to try and buy it is something I do not want to deal with so, kevraymond.com is born.

Honestly, it's the same as my Twitter handle so it works out ok in the end. Plus, it makes me sound friendly

So, the main site is a list of daily links I have read, found interesting and want to share. Each link may or may not have a comment or quote from me or the article. For referance, I won't put up a link unless I have read the article or a least skimmed it well (ya know, for those puff pieces). I hate random links.

Some days there may be one or two, others, maybe more. The whole thing depends on how busy the fabrication shop is. Everything is a work in progress and like I've said before, I want to use my brain and code some stuff, learning in the process. Therefore, things may change or have an issue here and there as I fix, research and ask questions about my problems.
