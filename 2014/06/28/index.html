Complete Fascination

I love simple things. I don't enjoy all the pomp and circumstance of things. Blogging in general has become so complicated and money driven. The biggest "blogs" of today consist of Huffington Post and Buzzfeed. These are full fledged online businesses, not blogs.

Fargo, even though there is a learning curve for me, is the coolest and simplest way to blog.

I recently, like a week ago, purchased a subscription to the new Ghost blogging service. A stripped down way to blog, which I like. Yet, now I feel like I may have to cancel and go strictly with Fargo for all my online posting and organizing needs.

Not only does Fargo give me the ability to blog, it fulfills my occasional need to code or play around with code. Plus, I can keep organized on personal and work projects privately.

How did I not hear about any of this before!? I read!
